  • Third-party use.

    • Expected collection and use

      Your Data collected and used only as expected.

      The website collects and uses your data only as you expressly allow or as you'd reasonably expect given the site's service. For example, when you provide your email address to an online retailer, a necessary and allowed use of that data is to confirm the order and provide delivery updates.

    • Unexpected collection and use

      Your data might be collected or used in unexpected ways.

      This website might collect or use your data in ways you didn't allow and wouldn't expect given the site's service. For example, an online retailer could use your email not just to confirm your order but also to send marketing messages without your permission.

    • For sale

      Your data may be sold or shared

      This website might sell your data or share it with third parties or affiliates without your express permission. For example, an online retailer could sell the data it collects about you (e.g., your shopping history, email or IP address) to advertisers or data aggregators without your permission.

    • Not for sale

      Your data is not sold or shared

      This website doesn't sell your data and only shares your data with third parties as you specifically allow or as you'd reasonably expect given the site's service.

    • Legal process required

      Your data not disclosed without proper legal procedure

      This website requires that government requests to disclose user data comply with proper legal procedure (e.g., the presentation of a subpoena or court order).

    • Legal process not required

      Your data might be disclosed without proper legal procedure

      This website might comply with government requests to provide your data even if the site isn't legally required to do so.
