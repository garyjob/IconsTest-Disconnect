Get Involved

Privacy Icons is an open project of the Privacy Icons working group.

The working group will convene each quarter to evaluate and adapt the icons and underlying legal text. To learn more about the working group, please contact icons@mozillafoundation.org.

We need your help to accelerate adoption of the icons. If you have ideas about campaigns, outreach, or other ways to bring privacy icons to the world, please join the project at Drumbeat.org.
